Dialogue Maker

What is this?

This is a tool to help you memorize your lines for a two person scene! In the name fields, type the name of the character you Are Not playing, and the character you are. Make sure you are second.

In the line field, type the first line of the scene. If you have the first line, type [scene begins] in line 1 slot, and your line in line 2. Make sure you always type your lines in slot 2. When the prompt line and your line are typed, press the "Add Dialogue" button. This will add these lines to the script! Keep typing the lines and pressing "Add Dialogue" until you read the end of the scene you want to review. When you are done, press "Next Line" to begin reviewing the script. Each press will either show the prompt line, or show your response! When you are done with a scene, press "Clear Script" to clear the way for a new scene.

Want to try out the tool without typing anything? Click "Frogs Script" to use the script for a scene from The Frogs by Aristophanes.
